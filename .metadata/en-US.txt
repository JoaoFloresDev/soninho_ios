## Description (4000 characters max)
Transform your nights and wake up refreshed with Soninho, the intelligent sleep tracking app designed to help you understand and improve your sleep patterns.

UNDERSTAND YOUR SLEEP
Track your sleep automatically and get detailed insights into your sleep phases including deep sleep, light sleep, and REM cycles. Our advanced algorithms analyze your sleep patterns to provide you with a comprehensive sleep quality score.

WAKE UP NATURALLY
Our Smart Alarm feature monitors your sleep cycles and wakes you during your lightest sleep phase within a customizable time window. Say goodbye to groggy mornings and hello to feeling refreshed and energized.

KEY FEATURES

Sleep Tracking
- Automatic sleep detection and tracking
- Detailed sleep phase analysis (Deep, Light, REM, Awake)
- Sleep quality score based on duration and phases
- Historical data and trends

Smart Alarm
- Wake up during light sleep for a gentler awakening
- Customizable wake-up window (15-60 minutes)
- Multiple alarm sounds
- Snooze and dismiss with haptic feedback

Statistics & Insights
- Weekly and monthly sleep reports
- Average sleep duration and bedtime tracking
- Sleep consistency score
- Personalized sleep tips and recommendations

Health Integration
- Sync with Apple Health
- Import and export sleep data
- Track heart rate during sleep

DESIGNED FOR YOUR WELL-BEING
Beautiful dark interface that's easy on your eyes at bedtime. Soninho respects your privacy - all data stays on your device and syncs with Apple Health.

Start your journey to better sleep tonight. Download Soninho and discover what quality rest feels like.
