## Description (4000 characters)
Soninho is your perfect companion for a restorative night of sleep. Designed for those seeking to understand and improve their sleep quality, the app offers intelligent tools that seamlessly integrate with Apple Health.

INTELLIGENT SLEEP MONITORING
Track every phase of your sleep: light, deep, and REM. Soninho analyzes your patterns and shows exactly how much time you spent in each stage, helping identify what can be improved.

SMART ALARM
Wake up at the ideal moment. The smart alarm monitors your sleep cycle and wakes you during light sleep, ensuring you wake up more refreshed and energized for the day ahead.

DETAILED STATISTICS
Visualize your sleep history in clear, intuitive charts. Track weekly, monthly, and yearly averages. Discover trends and see how your habits affect the quality of your rest.

APPLE HEALTH INTEGRATION
Automatically sync with Apple Health. If you use Apple Watch, Soninho leverages the sleep data collected overnight for even more precise analysis.

KEY FEATURES:
- Sleep phase monitoring (Light, Deep, REM)
- Sleep quality score from 0 to 100
- Smart alarm with wake window
- Detailed statistics by week, month, and year
- Intuitive sleep history charts
- Complete Apple Health integration
- Elegant dark mode interface
- Support for English, Portuguese, and Spanish

PREMIUM DESIGN
Minimalist and elegant interface, designed for nighttime use. The dark theme protects your eyes and creates the perfect environment to relax before sleep.

Start your journey today to better nights and more productive days with Soninho.
